What are constructed languages?

Constructed languages, or more simply "conlangs", are artificial languages made by so called "conlangers" for various purposes, like worldbuilding a fictional universe, aiding communication, as a logical experiment, or just as a hobby.

The most well known constructed languages include those of fictional worlds like Tolkien's Middle-earth (sindarin, quenya, aldûnaic, khuzdul, etc.), G.R.R Martin's a Song of Ice and Fire (dothraki and the valyrian languages) and Star Trek's Universe (Klingon). however, other kinds of conlang like esperanto have also received a lot of attention and space in pop culture
